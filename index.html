What is Explore Space?


Explore Space is a free, web-based collection of digital interactive and videos highlighting the latest developments in astronomy and Earth science.



Explore space gives you the opportunity to explore our planet, solar system, galaxy, and universe.


Interactives


Explore space interactives allow you to explore objects and materials from different perspectives,discovering how we can combine information to better understand the universe.
